## Workplace Procrastination Research Questionnaire

**A Message from the Researchers:**

Thank you for considering participating in this research study! We are a team of researchers interested in understanding the various factors that contribute to workplace procrastination. This study aims to explore how personality traits, time perspective, job characteristics, and leadership styles might influence procrastination tendencies. Your honest responses will help us gain a better understanding of this common experience and potentially identify strategies for managing it.

**Your participation is completely voluntary, and you can withdraw at any time without penalty.** All responses will be kept **strictly confidential and anonymous**. The data will be analyzed in aggregate form, and no individual responses will be identifiable.

This questionnaire should take approximately 20-30 minutes to complete. Thank you for your time and valuable contribution!
